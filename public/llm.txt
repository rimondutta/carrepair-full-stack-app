# Care Plus Auto Repairing

Care Plus Auto Repairing is a professional vehicle service center specializing in high-quality maintenance and repair.

## Core Services
- Engine Repair & Diagnostics
- Tire Change & Computerized Balancing
- Car Denting & Body Repair
- Battery Testing & Replacement
- Brake Servicing & Disc Blade Changing
- Windshield Replacement

## Key Pages
- /: Home page with service overview and company values.
- /services: Full list of specialized auto repair services.
- /contact: Booking and location information.

## Technology Stack
- Built with: Next.js (App Router)
- Data Source: Local JSON architecture for service records.
- Styling: Modern responsive UI.

## Business Philosophy
Factory trained mechanics, genuine parts usage, and computer-aided diagnostics ensuring 100% genuine servicing protocols for all vehicle platforms.
